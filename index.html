A ConsenSys Open Think-Tank Collaborative Initative

Problem

6.5 Billion dollars were raising during the 2017 ICO craze. How many of those had valid token models?



The Ethereum ecosystem needs an accessible foundation to design and develop new economies that are robust, equitable, and self-sustainable.  

About Us


TokenWork is an innovation-focused design processes from engineering, methods from economics, and best practices from business development all exist today. 

Web 3.0 entrepreneurs need a more accessible way to tap in to these resources to design and build cryptosystems.

We are a collaborative R&D and Think Tank initiative for token economic and business design.

Principles

Open-Source

Make token design accessible to entrepreneurs

Collaborative

Facilitate collaboration amongst stakeholders in the token design space

Self-Sustainable

Form a token design alliance

Self-Serve

Provide guides and self-service material to become standards in the space

Non-Profit Oriented

Become a center of education to spread and decentralize the basics of token economics and business design
